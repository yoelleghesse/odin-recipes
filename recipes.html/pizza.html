Pizza

The following is a recipe for making a delicious homemade pizza.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, onions, mushrooms)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine 2 1/4 teaspoons of yeast with 1 1/2 cups of warm water. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  3. Add 3 1/2 cups of flour, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, and 1 teaspoon of salt to the yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  6. Punch down the dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  7. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  8. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  9. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  10. Add your favorite toppings, such as pepperoni and vegetables.
  11. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  12. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
